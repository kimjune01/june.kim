## Standing constraints (the house style — honor these)
- **Ground by reference**: motivate by linking the prior-post knowledge graph, not inline re-explanation.
- **Citation tiering**: load-bearing claims rest on textbook/canonical results; companion self-links are texture, never warrant.
- **Specific invokes the general**: when a specific carries a well-known general, cut the general ("2+2=4 doesn't need addition explained"). Slack only when the general makes a thematic claim or carries a date the specific lacks.
- **Cut is care**: length left in is unfinished work offloaded onto the reader; cut re-derivation first.
- **No cult register**: sober; metaphysics stays in blog companions, enacted not preached.
- **Paper self-voicing**: no personified "the paper"; authorial "we" per convention.
- **One defended thing**: the trichotomy/entitlement redraw is the only load-bearing novelty; the frame is inherited and that is conceded (C4/C5). Synthesis is fine; "having epistemics is the flex."

- **Ramsey**: belief as a bet, strength = the odds (A1).
- **Modern re-groundings (surfaced in conversation, NOT yet in the paper)**: enactivism / Varela-Thompson-Rosch (knower enacts a world, dissolves subject/object); interventionist causation / Woodward + Pearl's do-operator (perturbation reaches structure).
