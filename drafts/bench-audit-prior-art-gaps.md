## Reviewer attacks (recurring across regions) + your existing defenses

1. **"Your audit instrument's own validity?"** — you already have Cohen's κ=0.52 on the two-expert tier;
   make it load-bearing. The mechanical spine (grep-reproducible) has *no* judgment surface — say that
   explicitly as the answer to "who guards the auditor."
2. **"Separate oracle-defect from contamination."** — you already do: model-free + Pro is
   contamination-resistant by design. This is your cleanest card; foreground that the failures provably
   *cannot* be a contamination artifact (no model in the loop).
3. **"Your bracket = known harness/infra variance."** — distinguish: those vary the scaffold/infra; your
   bracket holds the harness fixed. Cite Anthropic + SWE-Effi to show orthogonality, not double-counting.
4. **"Determinacy = OpenAI's 38.3% / Wang-Pradel's 29.6% re-sliced."** — the codebase-determinacy sweep
   is the answer: you certify the alternative reading is *correct*, not merely *divergent* or *vague*.
   State the rate is not recoverable from those figures.
5. **Power / N.** — you audited the full 728 (not a sample), which pre-empts "underpowered"; say N=728,
   full public set, no sampling.

## One-paragraph novelty statement (survives all six regions)

> Prior audits establish that agentic coding benchmarks misgrade — weak tests pass wrong patches
> (SWE-bench+, UTBoost), shipped tests reject correct solutions (OpenAI Verified; EvalPlus on HumanEval),
> golds fail their own verifiers (Datacurve; SWE-bench issues), and plausible patches diverge from intent
> (Wang–Pradel–Liu). All catalog *that* the oracle is broken. We show *what the brokenness buys*: on
> SWE-bench Pro — the contamination-resistant benchmark OpenAI now recommends — the above-baseline harness
> lift is **oracle-gated iteration, not reasoning** (oracle-free diagnosis and cross-model review are
> inert; self-audit goes false-green), and the residual losses are downstream of *solved* localization,
> arbitrated by a hidden test that silently resolves an underdetermined spec. We measure the
> underdetermination construct-validity over all 728 public tasks with a model-free, grep-reproducible
> codebase-determinacy sweep that *certifies* the alternative reading is correct under the materials a
> solver receives — distinguishing it from prose-vagueness flags and divergence-from-gold counts — and
> report a conservative two-tier proven floor (15.0%) with adversarial cross-family verification.
