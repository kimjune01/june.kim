4. **The silent-false-negative limit (applied).** Depth, the limitation that elevates. A detector only sees
   its catch pile; undetectable prose is silent, so a negative result can never reach "no workflow exists"
   (survivorship / Wald's planes). The blind spot shrinks with independent checks but never closes, because
   prose that passes every check looks organic to every check. Only a measure from outside the gate sees into
   it, and only partially. This is also the safety point: a model that learns to pass an eval is invisible to
   the eval; certification from a gate is trusting silence. Added as the closing limit before the provenance close.

   coming back to say "I think your gate is smarter than I assumed," not to complain. The instant "how was I
