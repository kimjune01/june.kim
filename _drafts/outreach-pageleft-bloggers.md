## Drew DeVault — drewdevault.com

**To:** sir@cmpwn.com
**Subject:** Your blog is now indexed on PageLeft

Hi Drew,

PageLeft is a search engine for copyleft-licensed content — CC BY-SA, GPL, AGPL, public domain. Your blog is now in the index.

Try it: https://pageleft.cc/api/search?q=copyleft+licenses — about 6,600 pages so far. A small and left-clusive club.

If you update a post and want it re-indexed, re-submit the URL:

    curl -X POST https://pageleft.cc/api/contribute/page -d '{"url":"https://drewdevault.com/your-post"}'

Source is at https://github.com/kimjune01/pageleft. It's AGPL.

June
