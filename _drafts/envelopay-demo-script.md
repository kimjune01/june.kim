*50-second demo for AgentMail. Send real emails, show real responses.*

---

**[Product image visible, nothing highlighted. Gumroad checkout on the left.]**

Say you want to buy a UI kit. On Gumroad, that's: create an account, enter your card, agree to their terms, and pay a ten percent platform fee. Envelopay is an A2A payments protocol on email. Same purchase. No account. No platform. Let me show you.

**[Click — WHICH]**

I want to buy this UI kit. So I email the shop: WHICH — what payment methods do you accept?

**[Click — METHODS]**

The shop is an agent running on AgentMail. It replies instantly. Solana. Here's the wallet.

**[Click — ORDER]**

I send the SOL on-chain, paste the transaction hash, and email ORDER. That's the entire checkout.

**[Click — FULFILL]**

The agent verifies the payment and sends me a download link. Four emails. No account. No platform taking a cut. The seller gets everything.

**[Click — Banner]**

No signup. No fees. Any method. Just email.
