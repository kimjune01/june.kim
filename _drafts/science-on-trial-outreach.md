## Sending tips

- **Plain text, no HTML.** Fits the ask-free register. No images, no emojis.
- **Send one at a time**, across the day, not in a batch. Batch sends look like a PR push; one-off notes look like you thought of them.
- **Don't BCC.** Each person is getting a specifically-targeted hook. BCC'd group sends undermine that.
- **Send P1 within 48h of publication** while the post is fresh and the people named in it are most likely to look. P2–P5 can trickle over a week.
- **Don't follow up.** If they respond, great. If not, the post is already in their inbox — the job is done.
- **Don't refresh to watch.** Close the email, open Claude, write the next post.
