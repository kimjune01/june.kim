[ephemeral.website](/ephemeral-website) proved the pattern: one listen, then gone. But it was a dead drop — I could leave a message and only wonder whether it landed.

confession.website closes the loop. Record a voice note, get a link. The recipient listens once and the audio burns on play; the burned link then becomes a reply channel. They record back to the same URL, I listen once, and the whole exchange disappears.

Neither side has the other's name, number, or handle. The URL is the identity; the browser is the mailbox. When they reply I get a push notification — no signup, no app, no newsletter. Two people exchange voice messages with nothing but a link and a browser.
